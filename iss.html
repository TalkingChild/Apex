Instant Self Serve

Instant Self-Serve (ISS) is a Web add-on for iHR and iLeave. It gives employees and managers the ability to check leave, request leave, approve leave, change personal information, change benefit enrollment, and manage time sheets over the Internet.

ISS provides secure Internet access to leave calendars, individual leave history, leave requests, individual time cards, and clock in/out features.
